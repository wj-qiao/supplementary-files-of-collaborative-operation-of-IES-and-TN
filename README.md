[Supplementary files of collaborative operation of IES and TN](https://github.com/wj-qiao/supplementary-files-of-collaborative-operation-of-IES-and-TN)

This repository contains the supplementary files of the paper entitled "Collaborative Operation of Integrated Energy Systems and Transportation Networks for Economic and Emission Management with Utility Theory", which is submitted for consideration of publication on *IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid*.

**IMPORTANT:** Two test systems are conducted to test the effectiveness of the proposed method, while just one test system is illustrated in the submitted manuscript due to the page limits. Another test system could be supplemented if necessary.
